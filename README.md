# 👋 Welcome to the Oracle APEX Workshop!

> **A free, practical, step-by-step guide to Oracle APEX — built for beginners who want to get things done.**

Hi there! Whether you've heard of Oracle APEX before or just stumbled across it — you're in the right place. This workshop was designed to be as simple and hands-on as possible. Every chapter comes with **real screenshots**, clear instructions, and no unnecessary theory. Just open it up and follow along. 🚀

---

## 🎯 Who is this for?

This workshop is perfect for you if:

- ✅ You've heard of Oracle APEX but never really worked with it
- ✅ You want to build real apps without writing tons of code
- ✅ You prefer learning by doing — not by reading 100-page documentation
- ✅ You want a guided, structured path from zero to a working app

No prior PL/SQL knowledge required. A browser is all you need to get started.

---

## 📸 Screenshot-guided chapters

Every chapter includes **real screenshots** of every single step — exactly what you'll see on your screen. No guessing, no confusion. Just follow the images and you're good to go.
